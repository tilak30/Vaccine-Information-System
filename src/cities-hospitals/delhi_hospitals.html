Delhi

Hospitals and medical centres in Delhi battling the covid pandemic and helping provide vaccines, medical care and guidance during these difficult times.

ANSARI NAGAR

Safdarjung Hospital

This is a multi speciality hospital that is located in Ansari Nagar and provides tertiary medical care to the entire capital city of India. It is also one of the largest hospitals in the country and is also a medical centre of learning

GURGAON

Artemis Hospital

Artemis Hospital is a state-of-art multi-specialty hospital located in Gurgaon, Haryana. This hospital is the first to achieve JCI and NABH Accreditation in Gurgaon. It is one of the most advanced hospitals in India.

AUROBINDO MARG

All India Institute of Medical Sciences(AIIMS)

This hospital is one of the best in offering healthcare services and treatment in the country. It consists of 1,766 beds that is currently, the largest accommodation in the country. This hospital also comes under the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare. AIIMS Delhi is one of the 3,006 vaccination sites across all states and UTs in India, where around 300 million priority groups are scheduled to get vaccinated over the next six months.

VASANT KUNJ

Fortis Hospital

Fortis Hospital, Vasant Kunj is among the top multi-specialty hospitals across Delhi/NCR. It spans across an area of 1,50,000 sq. ft., providing India's finest and skilled medical service to its patients. It is one of the NABH certified institute in Delhi initiated with the primary aim of provided world class medical facilities to patients.
